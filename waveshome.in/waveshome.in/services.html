WAVES HOME

A Smiley Senior Citizens Care strives to look after our elders like our own

Waves Home, a premier old age home, is one of the finest in Chennai. Our aim is to serve elderly citizens in a holistic manner, enabling them to live active, dignified, and healthier lives. Waves Home provides a comfortable and caring environment for senior citizens with essential facilities, including nutritious meals, AC rooms with SMART TVs and WiFi, 24-hour caretaker and nursing support, foot massage, and music therapy. Each room accommodates two members with an attached bathroom. Regular doctor check-ups and temple visits are arranged every 15 days. We also offer car/van transportation and laundry services (extra charges applicable) for added convenience.
