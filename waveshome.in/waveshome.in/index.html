WAVES HOME



A Mental Illness Rehabilitation Center

Inmates of the Home are provided with individualized & dormitory accommodation. Right now, 100+ inmates are in our Home. Individual attention is provided to each of them by our dedicated staff members. We have been running our MI Home for over 21+ years, with well-trained staff and regular doctor visits.

  2. State Commission for the Differently Abled (Govt. of Tamil Nadu) as a Psychiatric Rehabilitation Center under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016.
